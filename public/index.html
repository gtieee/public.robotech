ABOUT

RoboTech is a hackathon created for everybody. Robotics serves as a vessel for innovation in any field, allowing students from all types of backgrounds to join.

With RoboTech, we have divided Robotics into 4 pillars, which constitute our 4 tracks: Design, Body, Electrical, and Software. Students can choose to compete in 1 or more of these tracks. With each track, we have a unique format that will allow students to create whatever they imagine.

Check out some of last year's projects here!

TRACKS

DESIGN

Calling all Design Students, Business Majors, and Pitching Maestros!

Do you enjoy prototyping?

Do you have a passion for selling investors on your products?

Do you love market analysis and case studies?

Then the design competition is the perfect place to harness your skills. It is a Shark Tank Style Pitching Competition. Design a prototype using any software, present market analysis, feasibility, etc.

BODY

Calling all Mechanical Engineer Students, Art Majors, and Creative Enthusiasts!

Do you enjoy building?

Do you have a passion for creating art?

Then the body track is the perfect place to harness your skills. It is a Robot Body Design Competition. Design the body of a robot using any, and everything. We are just looking for creativity and feasibility.

ELECTRICAL

Calling all Electrical Engineering Students, Computer Engineering Majors, and Circuit Aficianados!

Do you enjoy circuits?

Do you like PCB design?

Then the electrical track is the perfect place to harness your skills. Make an project that utilizes a circuit. We leave the rest up to you.

SOFTWARE

Calling all Software Engineering Students, Computational Media Majors, and AI/ML Enthusiasts!

Do you enjoy coding?

Do you like working on innovative software projects?

Do you find Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence cool?

Then the software track is the perfect place to harness your skills.

FAQS

What are the dates of the event?

RoboTech will take place the weekend of March 3rd-5th

Who can participate in the event?

Any college or university students above the age of 18 may participate.

Is there a theme for the event?

Yes, robotics projects will be focused on mental health and wellness. Further information about the theme and judging criteria will be released soon.

What is the price of attendance?

The event is completely free to attend!

Will hardware be provided?

Yes! A list of available hardware will be released at the event

Will there be travel reimbursements?

Travel reimbursements will be limited to a maximum of $200 and will be subject to more rigorous application standards. Please note on your application if you are requesting travel funding.

Do I need a team?

While you may apply with a team already formed, you will also have the opportunity to find team members after registration.

Will there be prizes?

Yes!

Where can I go to get more information?

Please contact us at gatechieee@gmail.com
