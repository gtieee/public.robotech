About

  RoboTech is a hardware based hackathon run by Georgia Tech's IEEE chapter. Projects will provide participants the opportunities to use hardware systems like CAD tools and microcontrollers to create innovative solutions to real world problems.

  Competition themes and event schedules will be released as they are determined. The event will take place at Georgia Tech's campus from April 1st to April 3rd. While we are not yet accepting applications, please feel free to pre-register to get ahead in the process and hear new information as soon as it's released.
