ABOUT

RoboTech is a first of its kind hackathon. At its core, it encourages innovation in the field of Robotics by encouraging students of all majors to participate. There is something in the hackathon for students from different backgrounds.

With RoboTech, we have divided Robotics into 4 pillars, which constitute our 4 tracks: Design, Body, Circuitry, and Software. Students can choose to participate in 1 or more of these tracks. With each track, we have a unique format that will allow students to innovate.

TRACKS

DESIGN

Calling all Design Students, Business Majors, and Pitching Maestros!

Do you enjoy prototyping?

Do you have a passion for selling investors on your products?

Do you love market analysis and case studies?

Then the design competition is the perfect place to harness your skills. It is a Shark Tank Style Pitching Competition. Design a prototype using any software, present market analysis, feasibility, etc.

BODY

Calling all Mechanical Engineer Students, Art Majors, and Creative Enthusiasts!

Do you enjoy building?

Do you have a passion for creating art?

Then the body track is the perfect place to harness your skills. It is a Robot Body Design Competition. Design the body of a robot using any, and everything. We are just looking for creativity and feasibility.

CIRCUITRY

Calling all Electrical Engineering Students, Computer Engineering Majors, and Circuit Aficianados!

Do you enjoy circuits?

Do you like PCB design?

Then the circuit track is the perfect place to harness your skills. Make an project that utilizes a circuit. We leave the rest up to you.

SOFTWARE

Calling all Software Engineering Students, Computational Media Majors, and AI/ML Enthusiasts!

Do you enjoy coding?

Do you like working on innovative software projects?

Do you find Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence cool?

Then the software track is the perfect place to harness your skills.
