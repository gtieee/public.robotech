ABOUT

RoboTech is a first of its kind hackathon. At its core, it encourages innovation in the field of Robotics by encouraging students of all majors to participate. There is something in the hackathon for students from different backgrounds.

With RoboTech, we have divided Robotics into 4 pillars, which constitute our 4 tracks: Design, Body, Circuitry, and Software. Students can choose to participate in 1 or more of these tracks. With each track, we have a unique format that will allow students to innovate.

TRACKS

DESIGN

A Shark Tank Style Pitching Competition. Design a prototype using any software, present market analysis, feasibility, etc. Basically tell us by selling us.

Perfect For: Industrial Design, Architecture, Business, LMC

BODY

Design the body of a robot. No restrictions. Use Art, 3D Printing, Laser Cutting, Power Tools, etc. Just show us that it is feasible.

Perfect For: Mechanical Engineers, Artists, Biomedical Engineers, Creative Thinkers

CIRCUITRY

Design a project that includes a circuit component, geared towards robotics.

Perfect For: Computer Engineers, Electrical Engineers

SOFTWARE

Any Software Project.

Perfect For: Computer Scientists
